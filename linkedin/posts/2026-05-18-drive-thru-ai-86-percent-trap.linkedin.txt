Wendy's says its drive-thru AI gets 86% of orders right without human help.

Sounds great. But that's exactly where the next wave of enterprise AI goes wrong.

86% means 14% of orders required employee intervention — not necessarily a catastrophic error, but friction. And every friction costs time, patience, and (in the restaurant's case) revenue. McDonald's started in 10 Chicago locations in 2021. Checkers, Wendy's, and Taco Bell followed with their own platforms (Presto, Google FreshAI, custom voice).
