7-day intensive study plan

Designed for a candidate with technical base, ≥3 hours/day available. Calibrate this plan with your diagnostic result first.

Adaptive rules:
  • Any domain with diagnostic score < 40% → spend 1.5× the time below on that day
  • Any domain > 70% → compress to 1h, use saved time on weak areas
  • Pre-req score < 30% → insert fundamentals.html in Day 0 (+2h)

Daily ritual (regardless of day)

  • Morning (5 min): review 10 flashcards from flashcards.csv in Anki
  • End of session (5 min): 1-line journal — "today I struggled with X" → mistake-log
  • Evening (5 min): review 10 new flashcards before sleep (consolidation)

Compression tactics if you fall behind

  1. Skip Lab 1 guided → jump straight to speedrun
  2. Skim domain pages, focus only on tables (skip narrative)
  3. Replace second simulado attempt with focused flashcard sprint on weakest domain
  4. Never skip Day 7 mistake-log review — highest ROI
